Biography

Academic & Clinical
Excellence

Dr. Dilip Bhandari is a consultant neurosurgeon practicing at National Neuro Center, Maharajgunj and Frontline Hospital, Mid-Baneshwor in Kathmandu, Nepal. His clinical work spans the full breadth of neurosurgery, with particular focus on brain tumor surgery, awake craniotomy, and complex spine cases.

He completed advanced neurosurgical research training at Fukushima Medical University, Japan, where he developed the MORI system — a groundbreaking multimodal imaging platform for intraoperative neurosurgical guidance, published in Cureus in 2025.

A committed academic, Dr. Bhandari has authored 5 peer-reviewed publications spanning neurosurgery, public health, and global health, making him one of Nepal's most research-active neurosurgeons.
